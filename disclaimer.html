Individual Variation

Supplements affect different people differently. What works for one person may not work for another, and what is safe for one person may not be safe for another. Your health status, medications, allergies, and genetic factors all play a role.

Before Taking Any Supplement

Consult with a healthcare provider if you:

  • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have a chronic health condition
  • Take prescription or over-the-counter medications
  • Have allergies or sensitivities
  • Are planning surgery
  • Are giving supplements to children

Quality & Safety

Not all supplements are created equal. Supplement quality, purity, and potency can vary significantly between manufacturers. Look for third-party testing certifications.
